Humanitarian architecture has the potential to drive positive social change by improving humanitarian issues through architectural solutions.

This tool is designed to give you an overview of the various humanitarian projects around the world. It is in no way exhaustive, but rather meant to serve as inspiration for designers who wish to drive positive social change.

It's time to "design like you give a damn."

SHELTER

The necessities of shelter and forced settlements have become one of the world's biggest concerns, and the specialization of the architectural profession reacting to these needs is the essence of Humanitarian Architecture.

Click on an image to learn more.

COMMUNITY

Humanitarian Architecture has always acted as a catalyst to improve the well-being of communities - particulary those that are overlooked and underserved in society. These set of projects range from promoting opportunites for women to providing spaces for learning to recreational amenities.

WATER, ENERGY AND SANITATION

The need for water and energy is a fundamental one. Humanitarian Architecture can link entire societies to a better life by providing for these needs . These projects showcase just a small set of solutions that cater to the problems surrounding water and energy scarcity.

POLITICS, POLICY AND PLANNING

Architecture is an inherently political act, which is precisely why it is so contested. These projects are examples of how powerful architecture can be when it (re)constructs communities in a significant way and positively affects the politics and planning of a region.
